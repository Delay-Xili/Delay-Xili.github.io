Xili(Daley) Dai

daixili_cs AT 163.com; daixili.cs AT gmail.com

I am a Ph.D. student in HKUST(GZ), advised by Prof.Yi Ma of UC Berkeley, Prof. Harry Shum (former vice president of Microsoft) and Prof. Lionel Ni (president of HKUST-gz) of HKUST(GZ). My research interests lying on the intersection among closed loop transcription via rate reduction, 3D vision, sparse coding, and dictionary learning. My recent research focuses on 3D reconstruction via closed loop rate reduction.
